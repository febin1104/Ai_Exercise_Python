Bicep Curl

Instructions


1. Stand up straight with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a dumbbell in each hand. Your palms should be facing forward, and your arms should be fully extended down by your sides.

2. Begin the movement by bending your elbows and curling the weights upward towards your shoulders.

3. Continue curling until your forearms are vertical and the dumbbells are at shoulder level. Squeeze your biceps at the top of the movement.

4. Slowly lower the weights back down to the starting position, fully extending your arms.

5. Repeat
